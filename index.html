Welcome to Pan African General Trading Cooperation

Company Profile

Pan African General Trading Cooperation (PAGTC) is a Liberian company organized and existed under the law of the Republic of Liberia with it headquater located Carey and Johnson Street intersection 1000 Monrovia, 10 Liberia. The PAGTC Provides, Centralized, Purchasing and sale of global commodities directly from the factory to provide convenience and security to buyers world wide with their own brand called MaxLife. The company branding and merchanding of value consumerpack product in the domestic and international markets in over 50 countries world wide.

The Company, is a Pan Africanist business strategy involves building and intergrating business model which capture the entire chain of commodity from the origin and manufacturing, to the branding and distrubution of the value types of standard products of food and beverages including: Vegatable Cooking Oil, Rice, Wheat Flour, Spaguetti, Tomato pist, Sugar, Condense Milk, Fresh Milk,Powder Milk Sardine, Chocolate, Fruit Juice, Energy Drink etc. With our good partners and agent all over the Asia, South America and Africa as well. The PAGTC can procure and provide contruction material and export timber, lumber and log from Liberia to the world at large.
